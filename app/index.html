About the Project

Hello, my friend!
Do you want to find out about the project Wealth of Nations?

That is a good idea! It is because we created a unique strategy game. Our team implemented the simulation of the real economics of the Middle Ages period. Now our players are facing the same problems, which were nightmares for leaders of that time. This strategy is not only about the battle for the resources and territory with other players but much more.

You will fight with smugglers, corruption, the temptation of inflations and other tricky things, which you never saw in any other games. The invisible hand of the market has been presented in a maximum variety of effects. Therefore, the Wealth of Nations is the most interactive game in this genre. Don’t be afraid that it would be too tricky to play! Even the child is capable of managing the state because it is always possible to recruit advisers for complex tasks. However, these brats love taking  bribes.

The new mechanical processes forced us to make the prototype, where we checked economic theory in practice during the last few years (!). Check it out by yourselves, but don’t be too scared — the professional designers will join the team to create a commercial version of our game.

Play to earn

Oh, yeah. Just because this is a simulation of real economics, we decided to create an actual currency — tokens in our game. You can earn them during the playtime as it had been done in Middle Ages; by using trading, wars of conquest, diplomatic blackmail or even by boring gold minding.
Tokens will be traded on exchanges.

Let the best players make real money the same way as the Play To Earn  principle.

Motivation for the players to earn real money.

Tokens & NFT

The overall number of the gaming currency, “Ducats” will be fifteen billion.
We plan to release them on Solana by March when we will launch the commercial version of the  game.

Now we offer NFT tokens for you.
Our collection of posters is a part of the IGO (Initial Game Offering) sale.
Ten thousand posters of sixteen types are presented in total. They will be divided into four levels of rarity. The most rear one would be one of a kind piece.
Someone would be very lucky to get it at the time of sale.

The most important. The posters are unique, each of them has a surprise inside. After the start of the game, each of the poster’s owners can get from us an additional number of tokens, Ducats, which will already have market value by that time. You would need to activate the poster for that. Depending on the rarity of your poster the number of tokens will vary.
Not bad, isn’t it?

Do you think this is the actual surprise?
No.... That is only an additional bonus. The surprise is that the posters can be later activated to gain a unique gaming perk. It will give you a massive advantage to become a cunning opponent and help make money on it.
You can use the principle Play to earn starting from the purchase of the  poster.
